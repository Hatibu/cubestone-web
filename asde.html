Inventory management system(SBA Lite)

SBA Lite is a comprehensive software solution designed to empower small businesses, including shopkeepers, to manage their daily operations with ease. This user-friendly platform integrates essential modules for:

  • Sales Management: Streamline the sales process from quote creation to order fulfillment and invoicing. Track sales performance, manage customer accounts, and gain valuable insights to grow your revenue.

  • Purchase and Inventory Management: Optimize your inventory levels, streamline purchasing processes, and ensure you always have the right stock on hand. SBA Lite helps you avoid stockouts and optimize your cash flow.

  • Cash Management: Gain complete control over your finances with efficient cash management tools. Track income and expenses, manage accounts payable and receivable, and generate financial reports to ensure your business stays profitable.
